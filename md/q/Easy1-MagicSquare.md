# Easy 1: Magic Square

A magic square is a grid of numbers whose rows, columns, and diagonals must sum to the same number. Enter the number for $X$ which satisfies this magic square:
$$
\begin{matrix}
100 & 93 & 98 \\
95 & X & 99 \\
96 & 101 & 94
\end{matrix}
$$
